How your quote is calculated

Every line item follows the same transparent formula. No hidden fees, no opaque bundles.

Annual subscription = Base price × Units × Deployment × Region × Pricing tier × Contract term
Year 1 total = Annual subscription + One-time deployment fee + Infrastructure platform + Professional services
Factor 1

Base price per unit

Each clinical setting has a per-unit rate (per bed, per site, per patient, or per resident) that varies by module tier. The mid-range rate is used for all quotes.

Factor 2

Deployment model multiplier

On-premise and hybrid deployments require dedicated infrastructure, reflected as a multiplier on the subscription.

Hybrid×1.20

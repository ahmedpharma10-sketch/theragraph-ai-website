Pricing framework basis. Per-bed/year ranges from the published international pricing research (Sunrise Pharmacy comp: $100–$300/bed/year per ZipDo 2026; Aidoc / Bayesian Health ROI proxies; UpToDate; NHS framework signals). ROI anchor uses Pirmohamed/Davies ~£5,000 per acute bed per year of ADR-related cost — directly cited by the UK NHS and broadly transferable internationally. All prices are guideline ranges, not commitments; realised pricing depends on procurement, scope, region and reference value.

Phase discounts: Charter −50% (first 3 reference customers) · Reference −30% · List 0%. Multi-year discounts: 1y 0% · 3y −12% · 5y −20%.
